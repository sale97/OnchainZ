COURSES FOR BEGINNERS

Introduction To Blockchain Technologies

In this first course of the specialization, we will discuss the limitations of the Internet for business and economic activity, and explain how blockchain technology represents the way forward.

How On Chainz rates this course...

The course starts with the basic concepts and functionalities of blockchains. Later the presented topics devote lots of attention to less technical but to more economic and social aspects of the "blockchain revolution". The course is designed for beginners. The host is engaging and frequently challenges the audience to think for themselves. Highly recommended.

Introduction to Hyperledger Sovereign Identity Blockchain Solutions: Indy, Aries & Ursa

In this course, we'll dive into three Hyperledger open source projects--Indy, Aries and Ursa--looking at the tools, libraries, and reusable components they provide for creating and using independent digital identities rooted on blockchains or other distributed ledgers.

How On Chainz rates this course...

This is an introductory course and should be accessible to everyone. However, it would be easier to follow the course having a bit of understanding about blockchains in general and very basic information about the Hyperledger blockchain. The course covers a wide range of elements in detail. However, we feel it is important that course participants have a basic understanding of key elements prior to the course start.
